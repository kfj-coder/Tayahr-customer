# 📖 Case Study – Tayahr Ride-Hailing Platform

## 🧩 Problem
Transportation in the Comoros Islands lacked a reliable and structured ride-hailing solution.

Users faced:
- Difficulty finding drivers
- Lack of pricing transparency
- No real-time tracking

Drivers faced:
- Inefficient ride management
- Lack of digital tools

---

## 💡 Solution
Tayahr is a ride-hailing platform composed of two mobile applications:

- A **client app** for passengers
- A **driver app** for drivers

The platform connects both in real time to provide seamless ride experiences.

---

## ⚙️ Technical Approach
- Flutter for both apps
- Real-time communication with backend
- Maps & geolocation integration
- API-driven architecture

---

## 🚧 Challenges
- Real-time synchronization between driver & client
- Handling location updates efficiently
- Managing ride lifecycle states
- Ensuring smooth UX under network constraints

---

## ✅ Results
- Reliable ride-hailing service in the region
- Improved user and driver experience
- Scalable architecture for future expansion
